Privacy Policy – NotaFlow

Effective / Data di entrata in vigore: September 22, 2025

At NotaFlow, we respect your privacy and design our app to minimize data processing.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data to third parties. Your notes and reminders are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud synchronization, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple’s terms.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Subscriptions & Purchases

Payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple via in-app purchase. We do not access or store your payment details.

Data Retention & Deletion

You control your data. Deleting the app removes locally stored data. If you used iCloud, you can delete synced data from iCloud settings.

Children’s Privacy

NotaFlow is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Rights

You may at any time export or delete your notes within the app (if features are provided) or remove the app to delete local data. For any privacy request, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect app or legal changes. The “Effective” date above indicates the latest revision.
